# Checklist

The Strategy Checklist provides a structured verification process to confirm that all required activities, deliverables, and approvals have been completed before requesting the Strategy Decision Gate (DG-STR-01).

This checklist is intended for use by the Enterprise Architecture team, Project Manager, and Business Sponsor to ensure that the Strategy stage is complete and that the organization is ready to begin the Current State Assessment.

Completing this checklist does not replace the Decision Gate. Instead, it provides the evidence required to support the governance review.

# Readiness Assessment

The Enterprise Architecture team should review the following questions before requesting the Decision Gate.

| Question | Yes | No |
|----------|:---:|:--:|
| Is the business problem clearly understood? | ☐ | ☐ |
| Are business objectives approved? | ☐ | ☐ |
| Is the project scope agreed? | ☐ | ☐ |
| Are stakeholders aligned? | ☐ | ☐ |
| Are financial assumptions documented? | ☐ | ☐ |
| Are organizational readiness gaps understood? | ☐ | ☐ |
| Are strategic risks documented? | ☐ | ☐ |
| Are all mandatory deliverables complete? | ☐ | ☐ |
| Is the team prepared to begin the Current State Assessment? | ☐ | ☐ |

If any answer is **No**, the Strategy stage should be reviewed before requesting approval through Decision Gate DG-STR-01.

---

# Checklist Outcome

After completing this checklist, one of the following outcomes should be recorded.

| Outcome | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Ready | All mandatory items have been completed and the project is ready for the Strategy Decision Gate. |
| Ready with Minor Actions | Minor actions remain but do not prevent the governance review. |
| Not Ready | Significant work remains before the Strategy stage can be submitted for approval. |

---

## Summary

The Strategy Checklist provides a structured verification process to ensure that the Strategy stage has been completed consistently and that the project is fully prepared for governance review.

By validating activities, deliverables, stakeholder alignment, financial readiness, compliance considerations, and preparation for the Current State stage, the checklist reduces the risk of progressing with an incomplete strategic foundation.

---

## Next Step

Once this checklist has been completed, submit the Strategy stage for **Decision Gate DG-STR-01**.

Following approval, begin the **Current State** stage of the ENAF lifecycle, starting with **01-Purpose.md**.
